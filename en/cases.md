# Cases

Explore a selection of projects showcasing the application of remote sensing and geospatial analysis across natural hazards, infrastructure monitoring, and conflict assessment.

---

## Monitoring Transport Infrastructure Subsidence with Time-Series InSAR
Time-series InSAR reveals both regional subsidence patterns and localized deformation affecting roads, railways, and embankments in southeastern Spain.  
[Read More](./spain_transport.md)

---

## Detecting Airstrike Damage in Urban Areas Using SAR Change Detection
A multi-criteria SAR approach combining coherence loss and backscatter change to identify potential structural damage in Tehran.  
[Read More](./tehran_airstrike.md)

---

## Disentangling Earthquake Deformation from Multi-Orbit InSAR Observations
Combining ascending and descending Sentinel-1 observations to separate horizontal and vertical deformation components following the 2025 Aomori earthquake.  
[Read More](./aomori_earthquake.md)

---

## Tracking Wildfire Damage with Satellite-Derived Burn Severity
Using Sentinel-2 imagery and dNBR to quantify burn severity and map the spatial evolution of the Dragon Bravo Fire in Arizona.  
[Read More](./dragon_bravo_fire.md)

---

## Multi-source Remote Sensing of Extreme Flooding in Beijing–Tianjin–Hebei
Combining Sentinel-1 SAR and Sentinel-2 optical imagery to map flood extent and evaluate the strengths and limitations of multi-sensor approaches under heavy cloud cover.  
[Read More](./bth_flood.md)
